# Expected Season-Ending (No Return) Rates by League

These benchmarks should be used to validate our injury audit results.
If our data shows significantly different rates, investigate data quality.

| League | Typical % No Return Same Season | Why |
|--------|--------------------------------|-----|
| NFL | 25–35% | Short season (17 games). ACL/Achilles often end season. IR rules. |
| NBA | 12–18% | Longer season allows more returns. Many late-season shutdowns though. |
| MLB | 8–15% | Long season and minor injuries dominate. |
| NHL | 10–16% | Similar to NBA in schedule length. |
| EPL | 6–12% | Smaller schedule but fewer catastrophic injuries overall. |
